Balance Page

Seize the day

At the top of the Balance page, you can see an estimate of how many days ahead you are with your power purchases, or if you have used more power than you have bought.

It’s an estimate because it’s based on your current usage, so it will change as your usage changes, but it gives you a good idea of where you are at. Just like before, you can also see this information at a glance in the Power Organiser calendar.

Next you’ll see an estimate of how much power you’re using per day in kWh (kilowatt hours).

A kWh is the same amount as the unit we used to show. It’s the way power usage is measured on your meter, and many household appliances, so it makes sense to call it the same thing.

The Shop

It’s all about the days, and the dollars

The Shop is where the biggest changes are. Powerpacks remain all-inclusive, now covering fixed daily charges and any variable usage charges. Fixed daily charges replace the fixed costs that were previously included within unit prices.

Powerpacks are now sized in dollars, rather than units. This gives you a better idea of what you are buying.

Special Packs are listed in order of discount – biggest discount first. Underneath each Powerpack you’ll see:

  • The cost of the pack (what you pay for it), and how much it’s worth (based on your Standard Power price). This makes it easier to see what you’re saving. Extra-special special packs (like your monthly Simple Saver) will also show you a percentage discount off Standard Power. Making the savings simpler!

  • The cost of the pack is also shown in estimated dollars per day, based on your current usage. Dollars per day is the new way to compare the value of different packs at the time you buy them. It also helps you budget.

  • How many days of power the pack should give you, based on your current usage.

Both the dollars per day and the days of power of each pack will move around a bit, as your usage changes.

Keeping it fresh

We’ve freshened up our old Powerpacks and created some new ones, in different sizes to give you more choice.

Powerpacks no longer have expiry dates. Fresher for ever! So now whenever you see a good deal you don’t need to worry about whether you’ll use it in time.

Future Packs now come with a little discount – so you can save as well as spread your power costs. The earlier you buy a Future Pack, the more you will save.
