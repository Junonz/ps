Your property just got smarter

Understand how much you’re using and when you’re using it with our smart power tools.

Understand your usage

See what you’re using and when you’re using it.

Heatmaps show you the amount of power you’re using (in colour!) every 30 minutes of every day. At a glance you’ll see what you’re using and when you’re using it which can help you become more energy efficient - saving you money! You’ll be quick to spot emerging trends in your usage and what the impact of, say, leaving the heating on overnight could be. Eeek!

View your usage pattern

Compare your daily usage

No need to enter meter reads.

Your daily usage graph will look a heck of a lot more interesting now you’ve got a smart meter. Once the data crawls its way to us through the system (approx 2 days) you’ll see detailed daily usage data when you log in to your account. No need to enter meter reads and no more estimates. Bliss!

Check your usage pattern

Keep tabs as you go

Pinpoint what’s contributing to your usage.

Using the mobile app you can now add a comment or an explanation to a specific time period in your daily usage graph. This can make it easier to pinpoint a particular activity that contributes to a change in your usage. Spot the difference!
